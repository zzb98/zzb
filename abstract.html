Abstract of Course Project

Our project is a platform that aims to provide a user-friendly and accessible experience for patients to access healthcare services remotely. The platform is mainly designed for young people (around 20~30 years old), who are suffered from the subhealth.

The project will focus on providing a seamless user experience, with features such as appointment scheduling, video consultations, and medical record access integrated into a cohesive workflow. Usability testing will be conducted to ensure that the platform meets the needs of users and is accessible to all.

The project will also consider the ethical implications of designing technology in the healthcare industry, including privacy and security concerns. The outcome of this project will be a user-friendly and accessible platform that improves the quality of healthcare services and enhances the overall patient experience using the principles of human-computer interaction.
